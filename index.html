Welcome!

Welcome to the website dogs vs cats! Throughout this webpage you can learn more about dogs and cats, browse our gallery full of adorable and hilarous photos, read FAQs, learn more about its creators, and of course debate which you think is better, dogs or cats, on our forum.

This website was created for our Web 2 midterm project. We chose to create a pet forum since the backend programming would be a fun challenge and we each have our thoughts about what's better--dogs or cats--so we thought that creating a forum would be a great way to get different perspectives and start a friendly debate. This website has gone a long way since the initial brainstorming and planning stage. To the right, you can see an initial mockup of a content page and the forum page.

We hope you enjoy exploring this webpage and we can't wait to read all of the forum posts!

- Sydney (Project Manager), Fred (Lead Designer), Molly (Copywriter), and Maya (Backend)
